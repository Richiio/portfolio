Intro

Welcome to my portfolio! As a Junior Data Analyst/Scientist, I specialize in transforming complex data into meaningful insights. With my strong analytical and problem-solving skills, I can identify trends, patterns, and outliers in data to extract valuable information that can inform business decisions. My portfolio showcases my experience in data wrangling, exploratory data analysis, data visualization, and building machine learning models. I have worked on projects ranging from market and sentiment analysis to recommendation/classfication systems and natural language processing. By the way, check out my awesome projects.

Projects

Netflix Movie Recommender

The Netflix Movie Recommender is a project that recommends 10 Netflix movies based on a user’s input of a movie they once enjoyed. The recommender system analyzes movie descriptions, top movie casts, and other relevant information, and calculates cosine similarities of words to recommend similar movies. Click here to have a closer look at the github repo.

The Evolution of the Nigerian Super Falcons: A Women’s World Cup Data Analysis

In a bid to get off my comfort zone, I undertook an exciting project to study the evolution of the Nigerian Super Falcons, the national women’s football team of Nigeria, using data obtained from Kaggle. The dataset focused on the Women’s World Cup, and I specifically analyzed the Nigerian team’s performance over the years. Click here to read my write-up on the project.

Tweet Sentiment Analysis: Black Panther 2

I embarked on an engaging project to analyze the sentiment of tweets about the popular movie “Black Panther.” Using web scraping techniques, I gathered a large dataset of tweets related to the movie and cleaned the data to ensure accuracy. I then conducted sentiment analysis to determine the overall sentiment (positive, negative, or neutral) of the tweets. To effectively communicate my findings, I visualized the results using Power BI, a robust data visualization tool. To access the github repo, click here.

Data Science Job Salaries

Are you curious about data science salaries across the world? In this project, I explored a large dataset of job postings to uncover trends in salaries based on location, employment type, and experience level. Using SQL to clean and query the data, I gained valuable insights into how companies pay their data science employees. To help make this information accessible to a wider audience, I created an interactive Power BI report that received positive feedback from readers. Join me as I uncover the secrets of data science salaries in this Medium article!

House Price Prediction

In my role as a data scientist, I tackled the challenging task of building a regression model to predict house prices. Throughout the project, I faced various obstacles, including dealing with multicollinearity, selecting the most important features, and applying regularization techniques to improve the model's performance.
